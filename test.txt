Uninstall specific versions

Open PowerShell as Administrator and run:

Get-WmiObject Win32_Product | Where-Object {$_.Name -eq "Python 3.8.10 (64-bit)"} | ForEach-Object {$_.Uninstall()}
Get-WmiObject Win32_Product | Where-Object {$_.Name -eq "Python 3.12.0 (64-bit)"} | ForEach-Object {$_.Uninstall()}
Get-WmiObject Win32_Product | Where-Object {$_.Name -eq "Python 3.13.0 (64-bit)"} | ForEach-Object {$_.Uninstall()}

Replace the exact names if your installed versions differ.
